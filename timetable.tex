{\color{gray} The timetable for the 2026 Continental Sheepdog Championship is designed 
to provide a structured and engaging experience for all participants, judges, organizers, 
and spectators. Below is an overview of the key events and activities scheduled
 throughout the championship week. Please note that the timetable is subject to 
 change, and we recommend checking for updates closer to the event date.}
